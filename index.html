◆ HOW TO PLAY
1
IMPORT YOUR DECK: Paste your Limitless TCG 60-card list and the app builds your card gallery automatically.
2
FLIP THROUGH THE VISIBLE CARDS: Swipe left and right. Some cards are secretly set aside as prizes before you start.
3
GUESS ALL THE PRIZE CARDS: Use the autocomplete input to enter your guesses, then hit Reveal Prizes.
4
CHALLENGE MODES: Use the ⚙ to change prize count (4 / 6 / 8), add a time limit, or mix both for extra pressure.
5
TRACK WEAK SPOTS: After 3 sessions the app shows which cards you miss most often so you know what to focus on.
